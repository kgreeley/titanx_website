Why TitanX?

TitanX is designed to reduce supply, add programmatic buy pressure through smart contracts, and drive demand through game theory mechanics.

  • Massive buy & burn pressure: Small liquidity pools with constant buy & burn mechanisms make TitanX more scarce over time.
  • Large portion of supply staked: Users can earn massive ETH yield by locking up their coins, reducing the available supply.
  • Proof of Burn 2.0: Developers can use TitanX to launch their own tokens, burning TitanX out of existence forever.
  • Daily reductions: The amount of TitanX that can be mined is reduced daily, making it harder and more expensive to produce over time.
  • Leverage on ETH value: As ETH becomes more valuable, so does TitanX, due to its direct tie to ETH.
