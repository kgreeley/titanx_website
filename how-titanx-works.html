How TitanX Works

TitanX is a revolutionary cryptocurrency designed to create value through scarcity and innovative mechanisms. Here's how it works:

Massive Buy & Burn Pressure

TitanX utilizes small liquidity pools combined with constant buy & burn mechanisms. This approach systematically reduces the available supply over time, potentially increasing the value of remaining tokens.

Large Portion of Supply Staked

Users can earn substantial ETH yield by locking up their TitanX coins. This staking mechanism further reduces the circulating supply, contributing to scarcity.

Proof of Burn 2.0

TitanX introduces an innovative concept where developers can use TitanX to launch their own tokens. This process permanently burns TitanX out of existence, continually decreasing the total supply.

Daily Reductions

The amount of TitanX that can be mined is reduced on a daily basis. This makes it progressively harder and more expensive to produce new tokens over time, potentially increasing scarcity and value.

Leverage on ETH Value

TitanX is directly tied to ETH, meaning that as ETH becomes more valuable, so does TitanX. This creates a symbiotic relationship between the two cryptocurrencies.

By combining these mechanisms, TitanX aims to create a self-sustaining ecosystem that continually drives value through scarcity and demand. Join the TitanX community today to be part of this innovative cryptocurrency project!
